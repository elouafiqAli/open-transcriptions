The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said:
begin (charity) with those who are under your care; and the best charity is (the one which is given) out of surplus — narrated by Abu Huraira
References:
Sahih al-Bukhari: Book 24, Hadith 30
Sunan Abu Dawud: Book 9, Hadith 121 (Sahih — Al-Albani)
Sunan an-Nasa’i : Book 23, Hadith 0 Sahih (Darussalam)
